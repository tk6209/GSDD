# English: Executive Overview

### The Problem: Ungoverned Execution
In the race to adopt AI, most organizations focus on **execution speed** (Vibe Coding) while ignoring **governance**. This creates systemic anti-patterns:
* **Quality Degradation:** AI models lose focus in long sessions.
* **Lack of Auditability:** Code is generated based on implicit assumptions.
* **Systemic Instability:** Unsolicited side changes (Execution Bleed) contaminate the architecture.

### The Solution: GSDD Method
GSDD is a methodology designed to treat AI as a high-performance executor that requires **strict boundaries**. It shifts the focus from "how much we can generate" to "how much we can govern."

#### Key Strategic Pillars:
1. **Architecture as Law:** System boundaries are physical and non-negotiable (The Wall).
2. **Atomic Execution:** One task per context. No noise, no drift.
3. **Verifiable Contracts:** "No contract, no execution." Success is defined by objective criteria, not conversation.

### Business Impact
* **Scalability:** Move from individual experiments to enterprise-grade AI production.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Explicit governance prevents architectural drift and technical debt.
* **Predictability:** Consistent output regardless of the AI model version or session length.

> **Conclusion:** GSDD ensures that AI scales your systems, not your bugs.
